Return Policy

Last updated: 10 August 2025

At CABANA, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We understand that buying essentials online requires trust, and we stand behind the quality of our products. This return policy outlines your rights under Australian Consumer Law and our additional guarantees.

30-Day Return Guarantee

We offer a 30-day return period from the date you receive your order. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • In original packaging (if applicable)
  • Free from any signs of use, including but not limited to odors, stains, or damage

Australian Consumer Law Rights

Our goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

How to Return

Step 1: Initiate Your Return

Email us at returns@cabana.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll respond within 24 hours with a prepaid return shipping label and instructions.

Step 2: Pack Your Items

Carefully pack the items in their original packaging or a suitable alternative. Include all original tags and any promotional items that came with your order.

Step 3: Ship Your Return

Attach the prepaid label to your package and drop it off at any Australia Post location. We recommend keeping your tracking receipt until your return is processed.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned items, we will inspect them within 2 business days. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been processed.

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size. Follow the same return process and indicate the size you'd like to exchange for. Exchanges are subject to availability and will be shipped free of charge.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Items that have been worn or washed
  • Items without original tags
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Gift cards

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@cabana.com with photos of the issue. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund, including any shipping costs incurred.

International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error. We recommend using a trackable shipping service as we cannot guarantee receipt of your returned item without tracking.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us:

  • Email: returns@cabana.com
  • Phone: 1800 CABANA (1800 222 262)
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 9am - 5pm AEST
