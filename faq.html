Our products are crafted from premium modal fabric sourced from sustainably managed beech forests. Modal is:

  • • 50% more absorbent than cotton
  • • Naturally breathable and moisture-wicking
  • • Exceptionally soft and gentle on skin
  • • Environmentally friendly with a lower carbon footprint
  • • Maintains shape and color after washing

Our sizing is designed to be true-to-size for most customers. We recommend:

  • • Refer to our detailed size charts on each product page
  • • Measure your waist at the narrowest point
  • • For a snugger fit, choose your exact size
  • • For a more relaxed fit, size up
  • • Contact us if you're between sizes - we're happy to help

Remember, our modal fabric has gentle stretch and becomes more comfortable with wear.

To maintain the quality and longevity of your CABANA products:

  • • Wash: Machine wash cold (30°C) with like colors
  • • Detergent: Use mild, liquid detergent
  • • Avoid: Fabric softeners, bleach, or harsh chemicals
  • • Dry: Hang dry or lay flat - avoid direct sunlight
  • • Iron: Low heat if needed, avoid direct contact with prints
  • • Storage: Store flat or hanging to prevent wrinkles

Proper care will keep your CABANA products looking and feeling new for years.

CABANA products are designed for exceptional durability:

  • • Modal fibre is naturally stronger than cotton
  • • Reinforced construction at stress points
  • • Flatlock seams prevent breakdown over time
  • • Color and shape retention after hundreds of washes
  • • Average lifespan: 2-3 years with regular wear and proper care
  • • Many customers report wearing them for 5+ years

We stand behind our quality - if you experience any premature wear within the first year, contact us for a replacement.
