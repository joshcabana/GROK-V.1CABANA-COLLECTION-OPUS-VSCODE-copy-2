Care Instructions

Underwear & Bralette

95% Modal, 5% Spandex — breathable, soft, and resilient.

  • Machine wash cold (≤ 30 °C) with similar colours
  • Use mild detergent — no bleach, optical brighteners, or fabric softeners
  • Wash inside out to protect fabric and waistband
  • Do not tumble dry — lay flat or hang in shade
  • Avoid ironing or dry cleaning
  • Keep away from rough surfaces to prevent pilling

Towels

TENCEL™ Lyocell + Organic Cotton — soft on skin, strong against the elements.

  • Machine wash cold or warm (≤ 40 °C) with like colours
  • Mild detergent only — avoid bleach and fabric softeners
  • Tumble dry low or line dry in shade
  • Avoid ironing gold-plated grommets or embroidered areas
  • Wash before first use to maximise softness and absorbency

A Note on Sustainability

Our fabrics are designed to last — and to return to nature at the end of their journey. Gentle care not only prolongs their life, it helps preserve the planet they came from.
